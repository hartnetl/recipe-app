ABOUT

At Lord of the Recipes we aim to deliver a welcoming and inclusive space. The site has a simple motto - let the nerds eat! We encourage everyone reading this to sign up, create a recipe or share some love on a recipe you enjoy. We want everyone to have fun with the creations!

That said, we do have a few simple, but strict, rules:
All fandoms are welcome once the content being displayed is family friendly.
We do not allow swearing, bullying or offensive material on this site. All comments and recipes will be approved by admin upon submission to avoid content of this nature being uploaded.
While not compulsory, please consider compressing any images using sites such as this one. It will help reduce any potential slowing down of the site.
If the recipe you are uploading is not an original we ask that you include the link to your source - we are not thieves IRL. Any creators who have an issue with their content being distributed on this site will be respected and their recipes will be removed immediately.
Speaking of not being a thief, any images you choose to upload should be original or you should have permission to use them. If a creator reports an image being used without permission, the entire recipe will be removed immediately.

If you have any queries, complaints, feedback (or just fancy saying hi), drop us a message in our contact form. We'll get back to you as soon as we can.
